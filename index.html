research

PSS-Social — safe robot navigation in dense crowds

deep RL · robot navigation · CLAN Lab

A PPO-based navigation framework with density-adaptive proxemic reward shaping and dynamic collision-penalty tuning, targeting the freezing-robot problem. Benchmarked against DS-RNN, SARL, ORCA, and SFM at crowd densities well beyond prior work, improving success rates while reducing both freezing and collision failures.

[arXiv][code]

SIREN — selection-aware inference for adaptive LLM evaluation

post-selection inference · multiplier bootstrap · LLM benchmarking

A repeated-split reporting protocol that corrects the winner’s curse in adaptive LLM benchmarking, with simultaneous confidence bands on procedure-performance curves. Led MMLU-Pro tuning experiments across ten open-weight models (vLLM-served, DSPy prompt optimization), showing winner-based reporting can flip deployment conclusions.

[arXiv]

Core-Halo — decentralizing large-scale fixed-point problems

multi-agent RL · stochastic approximation · traffic control

A decomposition framework separating disjoint update ownership (core) from overlapping read-only context (halo), with exact fixed-point recovery under an explicit locality condition. Built the SUMO multi-intersection traffic benchmark and verified near-centralized convergence on PageRank and smart-grid energy management tasks.

[arXiv]

Inverse RL for clinical prescribing behavior

inverse RL · healthcare · in progress

Recovering clinician decision logic from large-scale longitudinal pharmacy claims for Type 2 Diabetes drug sequencing, comparing MaxEnt IRL, Bayesian IRL, and dynamic discrete choice — toward offline RL for prescribing decision support.
